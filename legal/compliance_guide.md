# Legal Compliance Guide

## Before Using This Toolkit

### 1. Authorization Requirements
- Written consent from number owner
- Corporate authorization for business numbers
- Legal basis under GDPR/privacy laws

### 2. Prohibited Uses
- Harassment or stalking
- Unauthorized surveillance
- Commercial spam/marketing
- Identity theft or fraud

### 3. Data Protection
- Minimize data collection
- Secure storage of results
- Proper data disposal
- Respect retention limits

### 4. Platform Terms of Service
- WhatsApp: No automated tools
- Telegram: Respect API limits
- Signal: No reverse engineering

## Recommended Use Cases
- Security research (authorized)
- Fraud investigation (legal)
- Personal number verification
- Academic research (IRB approved)
